About us
A Centre Rooted in
Knowledge and Integrity

Salama Legacy Centre is a Kenya-based knowledge organisation specialising in research support, professional training, and institutional consultancy. Founded on the belief that knowledge must serve humanity, we work with universities, schools, NGOs, and development partners to create credible, scholar-led solutions that transform institutions and empower individuals.

Our work spans research proposal writing, data analysis, thesis support, strategic planning, capacity building, and policy advisory — serving a growing network of clients across Kenya and beyond.

Vision

A leading African hub for research excellence, innovation, and institutional transformation that serves scholars, communities, and changemakers.

Mission

To empower scholars, institutions and communities through expert research support, capacity building, and evidence-based consultancy delivered with integrity.

Core Values

Integrity  ·  Excellence  ·  Innovation  ·  Dignity  ·  Impact  ·  Service
